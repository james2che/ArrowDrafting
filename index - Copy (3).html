Tropical Poles art gallery from Arrow Drafting and Design

Tropical Poles artwork is window art like stain glass in form but different in behavior with changing colors. It can be viewed with a particular color scheme as static art using the square polarized filter cover. Without the cover as kinetic art one can view changing colors while slowly rotating a hand viewer.

I have a unique interactive art that can be experience by all ages while rotating a viewer in their hands. The viewer is composed of photographic filter and here you will find options for viewing the art. The static version is a viewer cover that fits onto the frame of the artwork.

The kinetic version relys upon movement of a polarized filter disc. One assembles a stick handle and the disc. Then the stick is twisted slowly by the viewer.
